## Combat Basics

### How to Fight

1. **Encounter triggers** when you reach certain locations
2. **Attack button** deals damage based on your weapon
3. **Enemy attacks** between your turns
4. **Victory** grants XP, caps, and items
5. **Defeat** costs you some caps

### V.A.T.S. Mode

Toggle V.A.T.S. for:
- Target specific body parts
- See hit percentages
- Critical hit chances

### Healing

Use Stimpaks from inventory to restore HP mid-battle.

## Troubleshooting

### GPS Not Working

1. Allow location permissions
2. Check device GPS is enabled

4. Refresh the page

### Map Not Loading

1. Check internet connection
2. Try refreshing
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try different browser

### Wallet Won't Connect

1. Install/update Phantom
2. Check you're on Solana mainnet
3. Refresh and try again
